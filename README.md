# Oak Park Police Station Bond Cost Calculator

**Live site: https://jvanderberg.github.io/police-station/**

A property tax calculator that estimates how much a proposed Oak Park police station bond would add to your annual property tax bill.

Enter your home's market value and adjust bond parameters (principal, term, interest rate) to see the estimated yearly cost. The calculator uses Cook County's property tax methodology (assessed value, equalization multiplier, exemptions) to produce its estimates.

Built with React, TypeScript, and Vite.
